## LinkedIn Poster Agent Skill

### Purpose
Generates and schedules LinkedIn posts to promote business sales and engagement.

### Instructions
1. Read Company_Handbook.md to understand company policies
2. Generate engaging post content focused on business services/products
3. For auto-posting: Create draft_post.md in /Plans with content
4. If approved, simulate post (log only, no real API yet – for MCP integration later)
5. Update Dashboard.md with post summary

### Usage
- Call this skill to generate LinkedIn posts
- Posts will be drafted and placed in approval queue
- Approved posts will be simulated and logged
